Trolls



Trolls , small, happy creatures, are discovered by the Bergens, who eat them on Trollstice. They escape through underground tunnels, leading to their banished Chef. 20 years later, Poppy throws a party to celebrate, despite warnings from Branch. Chef kidnaps the Trolls, and Chef plans to overthrow Bergen Town in revenge. Poppy tries to rescue the kidnapped Trolls, but Chef takes them back to Bergen Town.
